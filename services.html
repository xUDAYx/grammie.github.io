About

Grammie is a connected community of elders and those who love them. Elders form the core of the community and seamlessly around them are carers who like an envelope, help simplify, secure and energize an elder’s life, enhancing happiness and joy in the lives of an elder. As in any community, our elders and carers share a beautiful relationship where distance does not matter.
